Who is Eligible for NDIS?

To opt for NDIS, you need to fulfill certain criteria, which is, you need to:

  • Be under the age of 65
  • Have a permanent and significant disability
  • Be an Australian citizen, permanent resident, or hold a Protected Special Category Visa

If you are eligible as a participant, you can go through a planning process with the National Disability Insurance Agency (NDIA). This plan focuses on your goals and the supports you need to achieve them.
