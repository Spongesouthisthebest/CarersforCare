Assist Personal Activity



What is personal care assistance?

Personal care assistance involves helping individuals with their daily self-care tasks. The goal is to support participants in maintaining their grooming and hygiene routines and navigating daily life as independently and safely as possible. Our NDIS personal support workers can physically assist with these tasks or supervise while you develop your independence skills.

These activities include:

  • Dressing and grooming
  • Bathing
  • Toileting
  • Personal hygiene
  • Shaving
  • Mobility and transferring within your home
  • Eating and drinking
  • Medication assistance


Choosing Carers4Care means choosing a partner committed to your well-being. We provide personalized care plans, highly trained support workers, and a compassionate approach to help you live a fulfilling life with dignity and independence. Our focus is on empowering you every step of the way.
