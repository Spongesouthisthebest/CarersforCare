Daily Tasks/Shared Living



Support with Daily Tasks & Living in Adelaide


Where We Stand Out

Assistance With Daily Tasks/Shared Living focuses on developing and enhancing domestic skills that allow participants to live autonomously. It can also be delivered within shared living such as units or host family arrangement.

  • One-to-one assistance with a carer
  • Assisting with daily tasks for independent living
  • Support for self-care, accommodation, and activities within a residency
  • Providing short-term residency
  • Arrange residency for transitioning to live independently

NDIS Shared Accommodation Services in Adelaide That We Offer?

Under our Assistance with Daily Tasks & Living program, we provide support with:

  • Personal care
  • Laundry assistance
  • House cleaning
  • Bed preparation
  • Medication management
  • Exercise and fitness guidance
  • Community access
  • Budgeting and financial management
  • Healthy food choices
  • Daily household chores
  • Meal preparation

Our aim is to help individuals transition through life stages with ease. Carers4Care participants by increasing their understanding of the purpose of funded support and enhancing their ability to engage in their communities by strengthening their connections with broader support systems. Carers4Care provides ongoing support management, answering any questions that may arise, to ensure participants understand their plans. Our short-term and long-term support services focus on enabling individuals to live at home, actively participate in their communities, and coordinate their support services.
